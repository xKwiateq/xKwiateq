About my work

A good, eye-catching logo is the factory of the brand's success.

Good logo is a factory to a company success. It's the first stage of getting customer trust and loyalty. This is why I will work with you to design the timeless, modern and professional logo for your business to succeed.

Timeless and Modern

Logotypes that I'm providing cannot be measured by time and don't go out of style. This fact allows you to have a strong brand image that will help you be recognized easily. My logo designs are collection of classic and trending elements, which combined together creates a brand mark that feels fresh.

Professional

An effective logo looks professional and seamlessly fits brand's identity. Good logo should be immediately recognizable and in the same time make you stand out from the crowd.

Video Editing

I provide video editing on youtube films, short clips, music videos, comercials, promotions. Professional footage edit, color grade and soft post-production.
